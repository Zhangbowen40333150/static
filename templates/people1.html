Hugh Jackman

Hugh Michael Jackman (October 12, 1968-) [1] is an Australian actor, singer and producer. He is well known for his role as the "Wolverine" in the "X-Men Series Movies". His performance in the 2012 movie "Les Miserables" made him the first Oscar-winning Best Actor Award nomination and won. Golden Globe Award for Best Music and Comedy Movie Actor.
